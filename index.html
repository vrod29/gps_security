ABOUT US

Since opening our doors in 2011, GPS Security has quickly become the LEADER in providing Security Services throughout the Rio Grande Valley.

We live in a world where the concerns for safety and security are escalating on a daily basis. From a personal or business perspective, these concerns apply not only to the well being of home owners, business owners, and customers but also employees, buildings, equipment, and inventory, along with intellectual property, tangible, and intangible assets. Whether the concerns are for yourself, your family, your company or event, our security specialists are the most highly trained professionals around.

Effectively dealing with these concerns has grown beyond the capacity of most companies. With limited time and resources to invest in security efforts, which are outside their core business, many companies have turned to professinal security firms ti fulfill their needs. GPS Security provides you with the ultimate in security and confidentiality you demand; thereby, creating the "Peace of Mind" you deserve.

In the arena of "Professional" Security Firms; our company clearly stands out from all the rest. So much so that we longer only service the Rio Grande Valley. GPS Security has now expanded to the Laredo, Alice and Corpus Christi areas.
